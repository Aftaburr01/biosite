About Dr. Ahmadur Rahman

Professional Journey

Dr. Rahman is an Internist practicing in New York City. He completed his residency at Newark Beth Israel Medical Center, building a foundation in comprehensive adult healthcare.

Today, his practice is recognized for its high standards and its role in serving diverse communities, including professional affiliations with international organizations like the United Nations.

Personal Life & Family

Outside of his medical practice, Dr. Rahman is a dedicated family man.

His commitment to his family and his hobbies, such as gardening and travel, reflects the same patience and nurturing care he provides to his patients.
